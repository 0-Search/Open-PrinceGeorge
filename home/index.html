Open Prince George

City council shapes your taxes, your roads, your housing, and your neighbourhood — but few of us have time to follow who's deciding what.
Open Prince George puts it all in one place, in plain language:
who sits on council,
how each member actually votes,
and where they stand on the issues.
An independent, non-partisan guide to help you vote with confidence on October 17, 2026.
