About & method

Open Prince George is an independent project. It is not affiliated with the City of Prince George or any candidate. The aim is simple: make council easy to follow for people who don't have time to follow it.

Every figure links to a public source — council minutes, the city's website, and Open North's Represent data. Found something wrong? Tell us and we'll fix it.
